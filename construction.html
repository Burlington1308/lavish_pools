Pool Construction

At Lavish Pools we secialize in building swimming pools, fountains and jacuzzis from the ground up according to the client's custom design.

We handle:


  • Excavation: Clearing the site and digging the pool shape, often by hand or TLB.
  • Structural Shell: Installing steel reinforcement (rebar) and pouring MPA concrete or installing a pre-moulded fibreglass shell.
  • Plumbing & Electrical: Fitting the weir, aim-flow, LED lights, and the pump/filter system.
  • Finishing: Applying marbelite or tiles, followed by coping stones and paving around the perimeter.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does construction take?


Concrete pools take 2–5 months, while fiberglass pools generally take 3–6 weeks.

What is the best pool type?


Fibreglass offers quick installation and low maintenance; concrete provides maximum durability and customization.

What is the most expensive part?


Excavation and building the pool shell are typically the most costly components.
